About Me

I'm a data scientist and machine learning engineer passionate about building AI systems that solve real-world problems. Currently pursuing my MS in Data Science at Northeastern University's Khoury College of Computer Sciences, I bring over 2 years of industry experience developing production-grade machine learning solutions across consulting, technology, and healthcare domains.

My professional journey has taken me from analyzing financial data at KPMG, to building recommendation systems at BCG, to deploying computer vision models in production at Airox Technologies. Each experience has strengthened my belief that the most impactful AI systems are those that seamlessly integrate technical sophistication with practical usability.

What Drives Me

I'm fascinated by the challenge of taking complex machine learning algorithms and making them work reliably at scale. Whether it's optimizing a neural network to run in real-time or building data pipelines that process millions of records daily, I thrive on turning theoretical concepts into tangible results. My work spans the full ML lifecycle‚Äîfrom exploratory data analysis and feature engineering to model deployment and monitoring.

Beyond the technical challenges, I'm motivated by the potential of AI to make systems more efficient and accessible. That's why I'm currently developing TailorTrack, an AI-powered platform that helps job seekers navigate the often frustrating process of career development. This project combines my technical skills in natural language processing and full-stack development with my desire to build tools that genuinely help people.

My Approach

I believe the best data scientists are equal parts engineer, scientist, and communicator. The engineering side ensures models run efficiently in production. The scientific approach keeps me grounded in rigorous experimentation and validation. And strong communication transforms complex technical work into insights that drive business decisions.

Throughout my career, I've learned that:

  • Simplicity beats complexity: The best solution is often the simplest one that works reliably
  • Context matters: Understanding the business problem is as important as building the model
  • Iteration is key: Continuous improvement and monitoring make the difference between a demo and a production system
  • Collaboration wins: The best ML systems emerge from cross-functional teams working together

Technical Expertise

My toolkit includes Python, PyTorch, TensorFlow, and the full ecosystem of ML libraries. I'm experienced with cloud platforms (AWS, specifically SageMaker and Databricks), and comfortable working with both traditional ML algorithms and cutting-edge deep learning architectures. I specialize in computer vision, natural language processing, and building end-to-end ML pipelines.

But tools are just tools‚Äîwhat matters is knowing when and how to use them. I pride myself on choosing the right approach for each problem, whether that's a simple logistic regression or a complex multimodal transformer architecture.

Job Search Strategy Plan

Executive Summary

This job search strategy outlines a comprehensive, data-driven approach to securing a data science or machine learning engineering position that leverages my technical expertise, industry experience, and network. The plan focuses on three key pillars: targeted applications, strategic networking, and continuous skill development.

Career Objectives

Primary Target Roles:

  • Data Scientist (Mid-Level)
  • Machine Learning Engineer
  • AI Engineer
  • Applied Scientist

Industry Focus: Technology (AI/ML products, SaaS), Healthcare Tech (Medical AI, Bioinformatics), FinTech (Risk modeling, Fraud detection), Consulting (Technical consulting, AI strategy)

Geographic Preferences: Primary: Boston, MA | Secondary: San Francisco Bay Area, Seattle, NYC | Open to: Remote opportunities

Timeline & Milestones

Month 1-2: Foundation Building

  • Optimize all application materials (resume, portfolio, LinkedIn)
  • Build portfolio website showcasing TailorTrack and key projects
  • Initiate informational interviews with 10 industry professionals
  • Apply to 15-20 target companies, attend 2 industry events

Month 3-4: Active Searching

  • Apply to 20-25 positions per month
  • Continue networking: 5 new connections per week
  • Prepare for technical interviews: LeetCode, ML system design
  • Attend virtual career fairs and company-specific events

Month 5-6: Interview Phase

  • Focus on companies in advanced interview stages
  • Conduct mock interviews with peers and mentors
  • Negotiate offers strategically
  • Evaluate opportunities based on growth potential, culture fit, compensation

Target Companies

Tier 1 (Dream Companies): Tech Giants (Google, Meta, Amazon, Microsoft, Apple) | AI-First (OpenAI, Anthropic, Cohere, Scale AI) | High-Growth (DataBricks, Snowflake, Weights & Biases)

Tier 2 (Strong Fit): Established Tech (ServiceNow, Datadog, Salesforce, Adobe) | Healthcare AI (PathAI, Tempus, Insitro) | FinTech (Stripe, Plaid, Robinhood) | Consulting (McKinsey Digital, Deloitte AI, Accenture AI)

Quality Over Quantity Approach:

  • Customized Applications: Tailor resume using TailorTrack, write personalized cover letters
  • Multi-Channel Application: Company website, Employee referrals, LinkedIn, Recruiting agencies
  • Application Tracking: Detailed spreadsheet with company, role, date, status, contacts

Networking Strategy

Northeastern University Network:

  • Connect with 50 Khoury College alumni in data science
  • Attend career fairs and industry panels
  • Leverage professor connections at Institute for Experiential AI

Professional Networking:

  • LinkedIn: Optimize profile, post weekly, send 10 personalized connection requests weekly
  • Industry Events: Attend 2 conferences/meetups per month (NeurIPS, CVPR, local ML meetups)
  • Informational Interviews: Schedule 2-3 per month with professionals at target companies
  • Referral Strategy: Target 40% of applications through referrals, leverage BCG and KPMG alumni

Weekly Goals:

  • Complete 5 LeetCode problems (focus on medium difficulty)
  • Study 1 ML system design case study
  • Read 2 technical papers on recent ML advances

Interview Preparation:

  • Coding Interviews: Master common algorithms, practice explaining thought process
  • ML System Design: Study recommendation systems, search ranking, fraud detection cases
  • Behavioral Interviews: Prepare 8-10 STAR method stories

Success Metrics

Monthly: Total applications: 20-25 | Informational interviews: 3-5 | Interview requests: 10% response rate target
